Best Fat Loss Solution

  • Do cardio every morning... with an empty stomach.

  • Find target zone. Use equation below...

  • 220 - age = 100% heart rate reserve. (196)
    hrr * 0.6 & 0.75 = upper & lower range

  • Personal Target Zone: lower-117.6 | upper-147

  • This is the heart rate zone to shoot for doing morning cardio.

Get Lean & Stay Lean

  • Don't care more about quantity of the calorie, focus on the quality.

  • Get active every single day. If you can't work out, do something.

  • Do morning cardio, in your target zone, with an empty stomach.

  • Cut back on the booze, very hard to cut those calories.
    Cuts motivation and you eat more.

  • Monitor your progress. That includes body fat percentage and lean muscle mass.
    Look into scales when you got the money. Be consistent when checking.

  • Create new habits. Take care of yourself physically... daily.
