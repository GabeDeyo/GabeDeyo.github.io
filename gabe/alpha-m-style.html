20 Simple Style Tips

  • 1. Never wear a tie and pocket square packaged together on the same suit

  • 2. Always go with a 2-button suit as your style

  • 3. Bow-ties are conversation starters

  • 4. Match your tie width, to your lapel width

  • 5. All khaki pants are not created equal

  • 6. Match your tie NOT to your collar size
    big collar, big knot. small collar, small knot

  • 7. Big baggy pants make you look short

  • 8. Big bulky shoes are dopey

  • 9. Your dress belt should match your dress shoes, in color and finish

  • 10. Avoid shirts with big bold logos

  • 11. Jeans with bling on pockets are no good

  • 12. Power players do not wear zany socks. Argile at most

  • 13. When suit shopping, size down.

  • 14. Remove the stitch where the vents are on the suit

  • 15. One ring per hand, no more

  • 16. Don't untuck a dress shirt

  • 17. Fitted doesn't mean tight

  • 18. Never match a black sport coat with black pants

  • 19. Take off sunglasses when entering any building

  • 20. Funny ties are not funny

Sunglasses

  • Wear sunglasses for a good accessory and prevent wrinkled eyes from squinting

  • Find face shape - I think I am an oval shaped

  •  For more circular or oval shaped head, lean more towards boxed glasses

  •  For more square or rectangle heads, try more round glasses

  •  For more triangular heads, simply don't wear triangle shaped glasses

  • For width - glasses will fit better if aligned with cheek bones

Cuffing Pants

  • Cuffing your pants is a trend "right now" and help show off sock game.

  • Only works with slimmer pants, never bulky

  • For a better sock game - Cole & Parker
